Spinach

Spinach is a leafy green vegetable known for its high nutritional value. It is an excellent source of iron, calcium, magnesium, and vitamins A, C, and K.

Spinach is low in calories, with just 23 calories per 100 grams, making it an ideal food for weight management. It is also packed with antioxidants that help protect the body from oxidative stress.

Spinach can be enjoyed raw in salads, cooked in various dishes, or blended into smoothies for a nutrient boost.
