Chicken

Chicken is a widely consumed poultry meat known for its versatility and high protein content. It is a great source of essential nutrients, including niacin, selenium, and vitamin B6.

Lean chicken breast is particularly popular among those looking to build muscle or lose weight due to its high protein and low fat content. Chicken can be prepared in various ways, including grilling, baking, roasting, and frying.

Chicken is a staple in many cuisines worldwide and can be paired with countless herbs, spices, and sauces.
