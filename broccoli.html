Broccoli

Broccoli is a cruciferous vegetable known for its high nutritional value and versatility. It is rich in fiber, vitamins C and K, and various antioxidants that contribute to overall health.

Broccoli has been linked to a reduced risk of cancer, improved digestion, and better heart health. It's low in calories, making it an excellent choice for those looking to maintain a healthy weight.

Broccoli can be enjoyed raw, steamed, roasted, or incorporated into a wide range of dishes, from salads to stir-fries.
