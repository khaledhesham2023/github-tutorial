Avocado

Avocado is a nutrient-dense fruit known for its creamy texture and rich flavor. It is an excellent source of healthy monounsaturated fats, fiber, potassium, and vitamins C, E, K, and B6.

Avocados are linked to numerous health benefits, including improved heart health, better digestion, and enhanced nutrient absorption. They are calorie-dense, making them a filling and satisfying addition to meals.

Avocados can be used in a variety of dishes, from guacamole and salads to smoothies and toast toppings.
