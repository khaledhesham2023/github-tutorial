Blueberry

Blueberries are small, nutrient-dense fruits known for their sweet flavor and high antioxidant content. They are rich in vitamins C and K, fiber, and manganese.

Blueberries have been linked to numerous health benefits, including improved brain function, heart health, and blood sugar regulation. Their high antioxidant levels help protect the body against oxidative stress and inflammation.

Blueberries can be enjoyed fresh, frozen, or added to a variety of dishes, such as smoothies, salads, and baked goods.
