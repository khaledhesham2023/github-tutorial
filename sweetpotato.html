Sweet Potato

Sweet potatoes are nutrient-rich root vegetables known for their sweet flavor and vibrant orange color. They are an excellent source of fiber, vitamins A and C, manganese, and several antioxidants.

Sweet potatoes have been linked to numerous health benefits, including improved vision, immune function, and gut health. They are also rich in complex carbohydrates, providing a steady source of energy.

Sweet potatoes can be enjoyed baked, roasted, mashed, or added to various dishes, such as soups, stews, and salads.
