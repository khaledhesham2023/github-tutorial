Apple

Apples are a popular fruit, known for their crisp texture and sweet-tart flavor. They come in many varieties, each with its own unique taste and use. Apples are a great source of fiber, vitamin C, and various antioxidants.

Did you know that apples are also low in calories, with about 95 calories per medium apple? This makes them an excellent choice for a healthy snack or addition to your meals. Additionally, apples have been linked to a variety of health benefits, including improved heart health, weight management, and reduced risk of diabetes.

Apples can be enjoyed in many ways, from eating them fresh to using them in recipes like pies, salads, and sauces.
