Fruits and Vegetables

Apple

Apples are rich in fiber and vitamin C. They are great for a healthy snack.

Banana

Bananas are a good source of potassium and are very convenient to eat.

Carrot

Carrots are high in beta-carotene and vitamin A, which are great for eyesight.

Spinach

Spinach is a leafy green vegetable rich in iron and vitamins.
