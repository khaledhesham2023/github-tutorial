Green Bell Pepper

Green bell peppers are crisp and mildly flavored vegetables that are packed with nutrients. They are a great source of vitamin C, vitamin A, and dietary fiber.

These peppers are low in calories and rich in antioxidants, making them a healthy addition to any diet. They also contain various carotenoids, which have been linked to eye health.
